The Challenge

Sales data that doesn't add up

Most organizations rely on multiple sales sources that never quite agree. The result: manual effort, inconsistent numbers, and slower commercial decisions.

Orders vs Actual Sales

What you ship and what actually sells at retail tell different stories. Without linking them, you can't see the full picture from factory to shelf — or spot where stock is building up.

Distributor Format Chaos

Every retailer and distributor sends data in their own format, product coding, and delivery schedule. Getting a like-for-like comparison across channels takes manual effort every single time.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked

What if our data is messy?

That's normal. The discovery phase maps exactly what you have. Our toolkit is designed for imperfect real-world data, not clean demos.

Do we need a dedicated technical team?

No. We build and hand over. Your team's involvement is reviewing business logic and approving matching suggestions — typically a few hours per month.

Can we start small and expand later?

Yes. Most engagements start with Tier 1 for one business unit. You can add sources, BUs, or add-ons at any time.

What happens after handover?

Everything is yours. Optional ongoing support is available, but you are never locked in. If you have the team, you can take it forward independently.
