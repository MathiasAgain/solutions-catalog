The Challenge

Sales data that doesn't add up

Most organizations rely on multiple sales sources that never quite agree. The result: manual effort, inconsistent numbers, and slower commercial decisions.

Sell-in vs Sell-out Disconnect

Shipment data from your ERP and point-of-sale data from retailers tell different stories. Without linking them, you can't see the full picture from factory to shelf — or spot where stock is building up.

Distributor Format Chaos

Every retailer and distributor sends data in their own format, product coding, and delivery schedule. Getting a like-for-like comparison across channels takes manual effort every single time.

What You Get

What life looks like after

The end result is a single, trusted view of sales data — refreshed automatically and ready for reporting.

One set of numbers

Commercial reviews, forecasting, and executive reporting all work from the same figures. No more "my numbers say something different."

Reports in hours, not weeks

Automated pipelines refresh data daily or weekly. No manual stitching, no waiting for someone to update a spreadsheet.

Self-service analytics

Commercial teams can slice and filter by channel, customer, product, and period — without depending on a data team to prepare each view.

Clear ownership & auditability

Every number traces back to its source. Business rules are documented and versioned. You know exactly what was matched, how, and when.
